The next million chains in the multiverse

Datalayer is a new layer 1 blockchain infrastructure that introduces localchains to bring infinite scalability of Web2 to Web3.

Introducing localchains

Datalayer revolutionizes blockchain scalability with its innovative concept of localchains, which are lightweight chains running in parallel on users' desktops, wallets, browser extensions, or mobile devices.
