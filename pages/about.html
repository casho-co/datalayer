We are Empowering Users, Democratizing Data, and Creating a Sustainable Data Market

Casho is a revolutionary consent-based data platform that puts users back in control of their personal data. Unlike traditional data platforms, Casho ensures that users are compensated fairly for sharing their anonymized data, allowing them to actively benefit from their own information.

Our mission is to democratize data by providing a permissioned and privacy-by-design environment where individuals have complete control over their data, and where data buyers, users, and society can benefit from high-quality and responsibly used data.
